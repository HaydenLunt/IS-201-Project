Hayden's Recipe for Pasta

  1. Ingredients
    • 1 box of pasta
    • 1 jar of pasta sauce
    • 1 pound of ground beef
    • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste



  2. Instructions for cooking the Pasta
    • Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
    • Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.



  3. Instructions for making the sauce
    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
    • Once the beef is cooked, drain any excess fat from the skillet.
    • Add the jar of pasta sauce to the skillet with the cooked beef and stir to combine. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.



  4. Combining the Pasta and Sauce
    • Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and return it to the pot.
    • Pour the sauce with the cooked beef over the pasta and toss to combine.
    • Serve the pasta hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
